The Mountain Boy

Byursulawest July 4, 2023July 17, 2023

On a high mountain, far up in the hills of a far distant land, a young boy lived all alone.

“Go and find your own kind, his parents had told him, but now they were dead and gone and still he had not left the mountain……….

“Where are my own kind?” he thought. “Who are they?

So off he went on a journey of discovery, leaving behind his beautiful garden and well-kept house.

“Look after the fields,” he called back to the wind and the rain. “I may be back.”

He walked and walked for many days. At last, he came to a town, a strange town indeed, for there, in every garden, were rows of paper flowers……
